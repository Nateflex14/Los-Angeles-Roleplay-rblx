Los Angeles Roleplay

Los Angeles Roleplay is a group for realistic roleplays. This group is a strict roleplay group, training is required for civilians and for jobs. None of are departments are affiliated with the real life department. There is no need for experience to join the group, all training will be provided. Training is mandatory for civilian and jobs. To join the group and be apart of the roleplay community, go to the discord tab, join the discord, then going to the application area to apply and wait for a response.
